About Daana.lk

Connecting hearts and creating positive change across Sri Lanka

Our Mission

Daana.lk is a Sri Lankan non-profit donation platform dedicated to creating meaningful impact in communities across the island. We believe in the power of collective giving and the strength of our united efforts to bring about positive change.

What We Do

  • Support education initiatives
  • Provide housing assistance
  • Fight food insecurity
  • Improve healthcare access
  • Strengthen communities
